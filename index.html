How to Play Pokémon Battle 2

Game Basics

1. Click "Start Battle" to begin a battle between two random Pokémon.

2. The battle is automatic - Pokémon will take turns attacking each other.

3. Watch the battle log to see what's happening.

Type Effectiveness

Attacks can be:

  • Super effective (2x damage)
  • Not very effective (0.5x damage)
  • No effect (0x damage)

Pokémon Types

Fire Water Grass Electric Rock Flying Psychic Ghost

Controls

- Click "Start Battle" twice to begin a new battle

- Click "How to Play" to see these instructions anytime

- Click on the "x" to close
